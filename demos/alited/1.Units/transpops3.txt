Now we see only branches of the unit tree.

To make <b>leaf units</b> we turn to our
customized options.
---
Thus, we make <b>leaf units</b> by the
pattern:

  # ========== <b>myProc</b> ==========

  # Processes *args*.
  #   args - optional arguments

  proc myProc {args} {...}
---
<r>Note</r>: <b>Ruff! doc generator</b> does
not fit to this style of doc comments.

Some <b>alited templates</b> might be
accordingly modified to mitigate entering
<b>procedures</b> and <b>methods</b>.
